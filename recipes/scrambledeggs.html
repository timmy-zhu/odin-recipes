Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs are a wonderufl source of protein, especially if you're on a diet!

With this simple recipe, you'll have yourself a healthy breakfast in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1-4 eggs
  • Cooking oil/spray
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat pan on medium heat
  2. Add small amount of cooking oil or spray
  3. Wait until pan is hot
  4. Crack eggs in and mix them with salt and pepper to taste
